About Us.

GH Mobile Limited is a telecommunications company providing first class services in the area of data communication, mobile contents, value added service (VAS) and broadband Internet service to the ever-increasing mobile network users in Nigeria and across Africa.

 

Founded by a group of young innovative Nigerians who believe the future is mobile contents and data communication. We are positioned to provide exciting and innovative data communication platforms and contents in Finance, retail, banking, agriculture, health, power, Education and entertainment industries that will improve business and lifestyle.

VISION:

To be the most sought-after innovative technology solutions provider for lifestyle improvement in Africa.

MISSION:

To deploy our arsenal of talents in creating and empowering our customers with services that will impact their life-style every day, everywhere, and all the time for the good of the brand- GHMobile and the benefit of all stakeholders.
